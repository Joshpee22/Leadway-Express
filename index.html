About Us

Leadway Express was originally founded by Stephen Wadler in 1956 as Modern Mail Service with the goal to meet mail delivery and messenger needs of the East Bay Area businesses. Since then, we’ve continued to grow and diversify our service offerings to meet your needs. We credit our friendly, reliable service as a critical pillar for our continued success. Now we are Northern California’s fastest, most dependable, 24/7 delivery service. Our courier services for the greater San Francisco Bay Area include same-day delivery, overnight delivery, route service, US mail, warehousing and distribution. We look forward to working with you!
